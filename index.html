About us

Our History

I have always had a passion for good chocolate. So when in 1985 my family moved to Ottawa ON, we partnered with the World's Best Chocolate Farm. This was the moment Coco's Chocolatiers was born. One year later we won the Award for the Best family business in Ontario and decided to open up our second location.

Everything in our store is hand-made, with no additives or preservatives. The recipes have been perfected over the past thirty years. Today we offer a great variety of baked chocolate products, chocolate bars, figurines, truffles and everything you can think of chocolate-wise. The success of our shop made our company be recognized all over the world.
